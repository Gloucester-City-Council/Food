# Food Hygiene Inspection Form

A digital food hygiene inspection form for Gloucester City Council Environmental Health Department.

## Overview

This is a browser-based food inspection form that allows environmental health officers to conduct and record food hygiene inspections digitally.

## Features

- **Inspection Details**: Record date, time, inspector information, and inspection type
- **Business Details**: Capture business name, address, owner information, and type
- **Hygienic Food Handling Assessment**: Evaluate temperature control, cross-contamination prevention, personal hygiene, and food storage
- **Structure and Cleaning Assessment**: Assess cleanliness, equipment condition, pest control, and facilities
- **Food Safety Management**: Review HACCP documentation, training records, allergen management
- **Temperature Readings**: Record multiple temperature readings during inspection
- **Overall Rating**: Calculate and display Food Hygiene Rating (0-5)
- **Enforcement Actions**: Document required actions and follow-up visits
- **Digital Signatures**: Capture inspector and business representative signatures
- **Save/Load Drafts**: Save inspections in progress and resume later
- **Print Support**: Print-friendly styling for physical records

## Usage

1. Open `index.html` in any modern web browser
2. Fill in the inspection details and business information
3. Complete each assessment section with ratings
4. Record temperature readings as needed
5. Select the overall food hygiene rating
6. Add any enforcement actions required
7. Obtain signatures and submit

## Files

- `index.html` - Main form structure
- `styles.css` - Styling and responsive design
- `script.js` - Form functionality and validation

## Browser Support

Works in all modern browsers (Chrome, Firefox, Safari, Edge).
